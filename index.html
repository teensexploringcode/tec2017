What exactly is a hack'shop?

A hack'shop couples the educational experience of a workshop with the crative problem-solving nature of a hakathon, meaning each workshop will be split between lectures and application-making.

Who can attend?

Participants ages 12 and up who have agreed to our Code of Conduct are welcome to attend! If you are out of this age range and wish to attend, please contact us.

We will have 5 different weekend workshops, which are designed to be followed in order, starting from simple syntax to UI, ending with a final project that addresses a real life problem on the last weekend. For more information, please refer to our schedule below.

How much does this cost?

Nothing! TEC is free of charge, thanks to our generous sponsors.

What do I need to bring?

Just bring your laptop!

How do teams work?

For the final project, students will use their skills and work with each other, with teams up to 4 people, on tackling a given problem.
